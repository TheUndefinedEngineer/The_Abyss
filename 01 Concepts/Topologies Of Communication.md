## Bus Topology

![[bus.png]]

- If a device needs to talk with each other it will cal the other device and communicate.
- If one device wants to talk to all other devices it will broadcast the message.
- If a device is disconnected it won't affect the bus.
- Used in CAN Communication.
## Star Topology

![[star.png]]
- 1 Master and all others are slaves.
- Master only can communicate and if master is not present the communication doesn't work.

## AD-Hoc / Peer to Peer Topology

![[peer to peer.png]]

- No master & slave concept.
- Only 2 devices need to connect and communicate with each other.
- Used in UART.
