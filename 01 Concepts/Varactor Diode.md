Internal Capacitance changes with the applied reverse bias voltage.
![[varactor diode.png|252]] 
- Varicap : Variable capacitor
- Varactor : Variable reactance
![[varactor symbol.png]]
![[working varactor.png]]
- Its a P-N junction concept acting as a capacitor, by varying the depletion region we control the capacitance.
	- Smaller depletion region increases capacitance.
	- When reverse biased the depletion region becomes larger decreasing the capacitance.
